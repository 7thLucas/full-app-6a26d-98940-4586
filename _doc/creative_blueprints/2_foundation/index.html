Why MoveOps Exists
⚠️ The Problem

Moves fall apart before they start

Moving company managers juggle bookings in spreadsheets, crew availability over the phone, and truck schedules across whiteboards. Miscommunication means jobs start late, crews show up understaffed, and trucks arrive at the wrong site — damaging goods, trust, and revenue.

✕ Fragmented tools — no single source of truth
✕ Double-booked crews and idle trucks
✕ No damage trail — disputes with no data
✦ The Solution

One hub for every moving operation

MoveOps replaces scattered tools with a single real-time operations platform — purpose-built for moving companies. Managers book jobs, assign crews with conflict detection, dispatch trucks, and track every move from booked to completed.

✓ Unified job, crew, and truck view
✓ Real-time conflict detection
✓ Damage log with photo evidence per job
