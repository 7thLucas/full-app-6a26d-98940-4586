# Product Overview — MoveOps

## What It Is
MoveOps is a web-based operations management platform built for moving company managers. It centralizes job bookings, crew assignments, and truck logistics into a single command center, giving managers the visibility and control they need to complete every move on schedule with zero damage.

## Target Users
- **Primary persona — Moving Company Manager**: Owns day-to-day operations. Books jobs, assigns crews, dispatches trucks, and is accountable for on-time delivery and damage-free moves. Works from a desktop or tablet dashboard.
- **Secondary persona — Crew Lead / Dispatcher**: Receives assignments, confirms availability, and updates job status in the field.

## Core Capabilities
1. **Job Booking Management** — Create, view, and manage moving jobs with full details (origin, destination, date/time, customer info, special handling notes). Calendar and list views. Status tracking from booked → in progress → completed.
2. **Crew Assignment** — Assign crew members to jobs based on availability and skills. Visual assignment board showing who is allocated where on any given day. Conflict detection for double-bookings.
3. **Truck Logistics** — Manage the truck fleet (vehicle details, capacity, availability). Assign trucks to jobs. Track truck utilization across the schedule. Maintenance status flags.
4. **Schedule Overview** — Master daily/weekly schedule view combining jobs, crew, and trucks in one glance. Highlights conflicts, under-staffed jobs, and idle assets.
5. **Job Status & Damage Reporting** — Managers and crew leads log job completion, note any damage incidents, and attach photos. Builds a record for insurance and quality improvement.

## Product Goal
Complete every move on schedule with zero damage — by replacing scattered spreadsheets, phone calls, and guesswork with a structured, real-time operations hub.

## Positioning
- **Category**: Field operations / workforce scheduling platform for the moving industry
- **Differentiator**: Purpose-built for movers — combines booking, crew, and truck logistics in one place instead of three separate tools
- **Tone**: Professional, calm, reliable — like a good dispatcher

## Branding
- **Name**: MoveOps
- **Primary color**: Deep navy (`#1E3A5F`)
- **Accent color**: Amber/orange (`#F59E0B`) — signals urgency and action without alarm
- **Secondary color**: Slate gray (`#64748B`)
- **Type feel**: Clean sans-serif, high legibility, operationally serious

## Scope (MVP)
- Job booking CRUD with status workflow
- Crew member roster and assignment to jobs
- Truck fleet roster and assignment to jobs
- Daily/weekly schedule board
- Basic damage incident log per job
- Admin dashboard with summary metrics (jobs today, crew utilization, trucks in use)
